Food Truck Sample Menu

Food Truck

Our team coordinates with yours to call the tables up by number, kind of like a really awesome made to order buffet.

Each guests chooses one side and one entree for their meal order takes approximately four minutes to be complete. Our team will take orders tableside and deliver orders to any guests that need special accommodations. Once the food is ready, guests have the option to spice up their meal at our condiment station that includes valentina hot sauce, sriracha sauce, banana ketchup, and our infamous house aioli.

We reccomend having one food truck per 60 guests

Cocktail Sample Menu

Cocktail Party

A cocktail party with us features a mixed of passed and stationary eats. This option is great for cocktail hour. A mix of stationary and passed hors d’oeuvres to kick off the event. Chef will work with you to customize a menu that best fits the needs of the venue, season, and atmosphere. This option can also be served as the main course.

Buffet Sample Menu

Buffet

A buffet station, our delicious food is served on paella pans instead of traditional chafing dishes for a modern look. This option is great for large parties, over 200 people.
